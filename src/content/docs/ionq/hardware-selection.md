## Available Systems

### Simulator (Free)
- Unlimited qubits
- Free tier
- Perfect for development

### IonQ Aria (25 qubits)
- Production ready
- $0.30 per circuit
- #AQ 25

### IonQ Forte (36 qubits)
- Advanced system
- $1.00 per circuit
- #AQ 36

## Selection Guide

Choose based on your needs:
- **Development**: Simulator
- **Production**: Aria
- **Advanced**: Forte
